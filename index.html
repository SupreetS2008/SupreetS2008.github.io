Our Cause: Supporting St. Jude Children's Research Hospital

A core part of the Flicks_Focused project is giving back. I am deeply committed to supporting St. Jude Children's Research Hospital. Their vital work in advancing cures and means of prevention for pediatric catastrophic diseases, while ensuring families never receive a bill, is truly inspiring.

While the primary focus of fees is to cover the operational costs of this community photography project, a dedicated portion of every client fee is also donated directly to St. Jude. By participating in this project, you're not only getting cherished photographs but also directly contributing to life-saving research and care for children in need. Thank you for helping me support this incredible organization!
