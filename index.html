About Flicks_Focused & Supreet Sudharshana

Hi, I'm Supreet Sudharshana, a dedicated high school student from Melissa High School with a strong passion for photography, technology, and community engagement. My journey into photography began with a desire to freeze unique moments and tell compelling stories through visual art.

Flicks_Focused is my initiative to make quality photography available to everyone in Melissa, TX, especially those for whom traditional photography services might be too expensive. This project is a way for me to apply my skills in Audio Video Production and Computer Science, give back to my community, hone my photographic abilities, and strengthen my portfolio for future college applications, particularly those focused on STEM and engineering fields where I aim to combine technical concepts with visual storytelling.

I bring a committed and detail-oriented approach to every session, ensuring high-quality results. I am proud to lead this project, leveraging my experience in:

  • Audio Video Production: Practicum experience in video production for football games and live streams.
  • Leadership & Organization: Vice President of SkillsUSA, Co-Vice President of Robotics, and President of UIL Math Team, demonstrating strong teamwork and problem-solving skills.
  • Technical Skills: Background in Python, Java, and core coding skills, enhancing my ability to manage digital assets and understand web presentation.
  • Community Engagement: Dedicated to providing accessible services and contributing to meaningful causes.

When you choose Flicks_Focused, you're supporting a local student's academic and professional aspirations while receiving valuable photographic services and contributing to a great cause.

Our Cause: Supporting Engineers Without Borders USA

A core part of the Flicks_Focused project is giving back, and I'm proud to support Engineers Without Borders USA (EWB-USA). As a student passionate about STEM and engineering, I deeply admire EWB-USA's mission to build a better world through engineering projects that empower communities to meet their basic human needs.

EWB-USA collaborates with communities to implement sustainable, community-driven engineering solutions worldwide. Their work directly addresses critical challenges in areas like clean water, sanitation, infrastructure, and renewable energy. This aligns perfectly with my own academic and career aspirations in STEM, and my belief in using practical skills to create positive change.

While the primary focus of fees is to cover the operational costs of this community photography project, a dedicated portion of every client fee is also donated directly to Engineers Without Borders USA. By participating in this project, you're not only getting cherished photographs but also directly contributing to vital engineering initiatives that improve lives globally.
